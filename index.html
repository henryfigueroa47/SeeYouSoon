See You Soon!

At See You Soon, we allow you to reserve your items at your convience, and pay for them within 30 days.

How do we work?

We Reserve

So before your favorite items go out of stock, we grab em and reserve them for you. So you won't have to worry about beating the holiday rush at the stores. Plus, since we are an online source, you can do this, all in the comfort of your home!

We Wait

We wait for you to be able to pay within the 30 days since the original reservation. Within these 30 days, you can pay however much you like, as long as the by the 30 days the item is payed in full. Plus the deals that the item had when you reserved it stay, so you can save money too!

You Enjoy

Then after the 30 days, you have the chance to claim your items. Then you can give your family, your friends, or even yourself that wonderful present that they've been waiting for.
