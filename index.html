See You Soon!

At See You Soon, we allow you to reserve your items at your convience, and pay for them within 30 days.

How do we work?

We Reserve your favorite items!

So before your favorite items go out of stock, we grab em and reserve them for you. So you won't have to worry about beating the holiday rush at the stores. However, must pay a non-refundable desposit of 10% of the item's price. Plus, since we are an online source, you can do this, all in the comfort of your home!

Within 90 days you can gradually pay for it.

We wait for you to be able to pay within the 90 days since the original reservation. Within these 90 days, you can pay however much you like, as long as the by the 90 days the item is payed in full. Plus the deals that the item had when you reserved it stay, so you can save money too!

We Ship the item to you!

Then after the 90 days, you have the chance to claim your items. Then you can give your family, your friends, or even yourself that wonderful present that they've been waiting for.
